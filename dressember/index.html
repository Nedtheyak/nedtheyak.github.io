Dressember 2021

It's time for Dressember again!

Last year I wore a tie all day every day in December, and this year I will again! We're raising awareness and money for the International Justice Mission (IJM). They work to rescue people from human trafficking and slavery, and have been doing so for over 20 years.

There are more than 40 million people being trafficked right now, and we can change that.
